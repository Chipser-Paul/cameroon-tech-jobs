Your Privacy Matters

At CameroonTechJobs, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

For Job Seekers:

  • Full name, email address, phone number
  • Professional profile (bio, experience, skills)
  • Profile photo (stored securely on Cloudinary)
  • Work history and education details
  • Social media links (GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolio)
  • Job applications and cover notes
  • Communication with employers

For Companies:

  • Company name, email address, phone number
  • Company logo and description
  • Website URL and location
  • Job postings and requirements
  • Payment information (processed by Tranzak)
  • Communication with applicants

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on platform
  • Device type and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display job listings and candidate profiles
  • Facilitate job applications and employer-candidate communication
  • Process payments for job postings
  • Send job alerts and platform notifications
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Storage Location: Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on Render (cloud infrastructure) and Cloudinary (for images). All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS.

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices including password hashing, secure authentication, and regular security updates.

Payment Data: We do NOT store your payment card information. All payments are processed securely by Tranzak, a licensed payment processor.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We only share data in these circumstances:

  • With Employers: When you apply for a job, your profile and application are shared with the posting company
  • With Job Seekers: Company information and job details are publicly visible on the platform
  • With Service Providers: Tranzak (payments), Cloudinary (images), Render (hosting) - only data necessary for their services
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Cameroonian law or court order

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (contact us)
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at chipseremmanuel@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Job postings are anonymized or removed
  • Payment records are retained for 5 years (legal requirement)
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely

8. Third-Party Services

Our platform uses:

  • Tranzak: Payment processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudinary: Image storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Render: Hosting infrastructure (Privacy Policy)

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notification. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: chipseremmanuel@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +237 675 952 537
  • Location: Douala, Cameroon

We respond to privacy requests within 48 hours.

By using CameroonTechJobs, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.
