Privacy Policy

How CameroonTechJobs handles user and employer data.

We collect the information needed to operate the hiring platform, including account details, listing content, application data, and communication records created inside the product.

What we collect

This may include names, email addresses, phone numbers, company profiles, candidate profiles, application content, interview details, and payment references.

How we use it

We use your data to run accounts, publish listings, deliver applications, process payments, send operational notifications, and improve platform reliability.

Payment processing

Payment transactions are handled by our checkout provider. We store payment references and status information needed for activation, support, and reconciliation.

Data sharing

We share candidate data with employers only as part of the application and hiring workflow, and we share operational data with service providers only when needed to run the platform.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent through the contact page.
