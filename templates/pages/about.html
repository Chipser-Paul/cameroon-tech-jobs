About CameroonTechJobs

Built in Douala, for Cameroon's growing tech community.

🎯

Our Mission

CameroonTechJobs exists to bridge the gap between talented IT professionals and the companies that need them — right here in Cameroon. We believe that local tech talent deserves a dedicated platform built specifically for our market, our culture, and our payment methods.

🇨🇲

Our Story

We are a team of software engineers based in Douala who experienced firsthand how difficult it was to find quality IT jobs and tech talent in Cameroon. Generic job boards weren't built for us — they don't understand our cities, our payment systems, or our tech ecosystem.

So we built CameroonTechJobs — a platform that speaks our language, accepts Mobile Money, and focuses 100% on the tech sector across Douala, Yaoundé, Bamenda, Bafoussam and beyond.
