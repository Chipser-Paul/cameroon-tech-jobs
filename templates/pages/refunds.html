Refund Policy

How refunds and payment disputes are handled for paid listings.

As a general rule, paid listing fees are non-refundable once payment has been confirmed and the listing has been activated on the platform.

Eligible cases

We may review refund requests in limited cases such as duplicate charges, confirmed technical errors that prevented activation, or billing mistakes caused by the platform.

Non-refundable cases

We generally do not refund listings that were correctly activated, used, closed by the employer, or purchased by mistake after successful confirmation on the checkout page.

How to request review

To request a refund review, contact us promptly with your company name, payment reference, listing title, and a short explanation of the issue.

Support

Send refund questions through the contact page so we can review the payment record.
