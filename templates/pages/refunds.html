Our Commitment to Fair Refunds

We understand that circumstances change. This policy outlines when and how refunds are processed for payments made on CameroonTechJobs.

1. Free Listing Policy

The first job listing for each company is completely free. No payment is required, and therefore no refunds apply.

2. Paid Listing Refunds

✅ Full Refund Eligibility:

You are eligible for a 100% refund if:

  • Payment was charged but job was never activated due to our technical error
  • Job was activated but removed by us within 24 hours due to policy violation (no refund for policy violations)
  • Duplicate charge occurred due to system error
  • Payment was processed but Tranzak transaction failed

⚠️ Partial Refund Eligibility:

You may receive a pro-rated refund if:

  • You request cancellation within 7 days of purchase (unused days refunded)
  • Platform downtime exceeds 48 consecutive hours during your listing period
  • Significant feature malfunction prevents job visibility (verified by our team)

Calculation: Refund = (Amount Paid ÷ Total Days) × Remaining Days

❌ No Refund Eligibility:

Refunds are NOT provided for:

  • Changed mind after job activation
  • Low number of applicants (we do not guarantee hiring outcomes)
  • Job expired after the agreed duration (30 or 60 days)
  • Company closed the job voluntarily
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • More than 7 days have passed since purchase
  • Incorrect information provided during job posting

3. Refund Request Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us via email (chipseremmanuel@gmail.com) or WhatsApp (+237 675 952 537)
  2. Include your company name, job title, and transaction reference ID
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. We will review your request within 48 hours
  5. If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

4. Refund Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

  • MTN Mobile Money: Refunded to the same mobile money account
  • Orange Money: Refunded to the same mobile money account
  • Bank Transfer: Refunded to the same bank account (may take 3-5 additional business days)

Processing fees charged by Tranzak may be deducted from partial refunds.

5. Platform Downtime Compensation

If CameroonTechJobs experiences unplanned downtime exceeding 48 consecutive hours:

  • You will receive automatic listing extension equal to the downtime period
  • Alternatively, you may request a pro-rated refund
  • Scheduled maintenance (announced 24 hours in advance) is not eligible

6. Disputed Charges

If you believe you were charged incorrectly:

  1. Contact us first - we can often resolve issues faster than chargebacks
  2. Provide transaction details and payment receipt
  3. We will investigate and respond within 48 hours
  4. If unresolved, you may file a dispute with Tranzak or your payment provider

7. Account Termination

If your account is terminated due to Terms of Service violations:

  • Active job listings will be removed
  • No refunds will be issued for unused listing time
  • Outstanding balances remain due

8. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions:

  • Email: chipseremmanuel@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +237 675 952 537
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
  • Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (GMT+1)

Important Note

We strive to provide excellent service and fair refund practices. If you're unsatisfied, please contact us before requesting a chargeback - we're committed to finding a solution that works for both parties.
